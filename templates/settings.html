Take Profit & Stop Loss Settings
How it works

When enabled, the system will automatically place TP/SL orders for each new position:

  • Take Profit: Price target for securing profits, calculated as a percentage from entry price
  • Stop Loss: Price limit for minimizing losses, calculated as a percentage from entry price
When enabled, TP/SL orders will be placed automatically with each new position
